About Me

I am a third-year PhD student in the IPRoBe Lab at Michigan State University under the supervision of Dr. Arun Ross. My academic background includes a bachelor’s degree in Mathematics and a master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering.

My research interests include biometrics, facial enhancement, video frame selection, and multimodal fusion of face and body biometric data. I am currently developing AI-based frameworks to support cosmetic surgery procedures and facial aesthetics enhancement.
