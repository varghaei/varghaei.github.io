I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Michigan State University and a member of the research group led by Dr. Arun Ross. My work spans biometrics, facial analysis, multimodal learning, and AI methods for aesthetic and medical image understanding.

My recent research includes low-resolution person recognition, multimodal fusion of face and body cues, automated assessment of facial plastic surgery outcomes, and detection of manipulated or edited faces using vision-language and robustness-based methods.
